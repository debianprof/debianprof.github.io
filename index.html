Phase Shifting Algorithm

Consider four phase shifted frames.

\[ \begin{align*} I_1(x,y)&=a(x,y)+b(x,y)\cos[\phi(x,y)]\\ I_2(x,y)&=a(x,y)+b(x,y)\cos[\phi(x,y)+\pi/2]\\ I_3(x,y)&=a(x,y)+b(x,y)\cos[\phi(x,y)+\pi]\\ I_4(x,y)&=a(x,y)+b(x,y)\cos[\phi(x,y)+3\pi/2] \end{align*} \]
